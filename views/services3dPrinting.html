After receiving your our, we will quickly analyse it and get it quoted. After quotation, you will be given intructions regarding how to pay the quoted amount.

3D Printing of Order

After we receive and confirm your payment, the countdown for the 3D printing of your order begins. There are three pricing options available: Basic, Priority and Urgent. Pricing determines the duration of the printing time of your order.

Basic is free-of-charge and has a 3D printing time of up to 5 days. Priority raises the order price by 25% and has a 3D printing time of up to 3 days. Urgent raises the order price by 50% and has a 3D printing time of 1 day. Depending on the availability of 3D printers, the 3D printing of orders can be completed ahead of it's specified due date.

Order Delivery

After we 3D print your order, you can either pickup your order from our facility or get it delivered to you. The delivery option must be specified during placing of an order. The available delivery options are 'Pickup', 'Tracking' and 'Courier'.

Pickup is free-of-charge and can be arrange at any day and time of the week. Tracking costs $7.00 and will take 1-3 working days. Courier costs $8.50 and will take 1 working day. Both Tracking and Courier allow customers to track their shipment.
