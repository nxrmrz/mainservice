Partnership Program

The Delta 3D Printing Partnership Program provides you with a range of benefits to ensure delivery of great 3D printing services that are tailored to your own needs.

Co-Creation

We'll work with you anywhere from design stage all the way through manufacturing stage to ensure quality and compatibility of your products for 3D printing. We can start from scratch or re-design a product to suit 3D printing manufacturing.

Discounts on Services

Partners get an automatic 20% discount for 3D Printing Services. Additional discounts will be applied based on your previous month's cumulative order value. Partners 3D printing orders will be prioritised without any additional charges.

Explore the discounts

Personalisation of Services

We'll work with you to determine the best way to deliver the 3D printing services to suit your own practice goals. Example of this could be setting a daily/weekly production rate and delivery date of your 3D printed products.

Becoming a Partner

Becoming a partner is simple! You just need a minimum average monthly cumulative order value of $1,000. This can be based on your previous order history with us or based on a forecast.
