- Our Story -

At Delta 3D Printing, we aim to build manufacturing facilities which utilise 3D printing technologies to empower businesses, creators and hobbyists. A place where businesses can outsource their manufacturing in a cost-effective way while being able to produce high-quality products.

We understand that manufacturing is costly, it requires investments in machines, maintenance, employees and more. We, at Delta 3D Printing, will take care of these costly investments and activities for you.

We also focus on building relationships with our partners to ensure that the services we provide is personalised to their specifications and needs. We want to make manufacturing of high-quality products affordable to every businesses regardless of its size (not just the top 5%).

Our Mission

To make manufacturing of high-quality products affordable and available to everyone through the use of 3D printing technologies.
