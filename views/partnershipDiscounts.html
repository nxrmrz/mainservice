Partner Discounts

Do more for less the price!

15% Discount

Partners receives an automatic 15% discount for all of their 3D printing orders.

Monthly Discounts

Additional discount for the month is applied based on the previous month's cumulative order value.

Previous Month's Cumulative Order Value Additional Discount
$2000 +5%
$3000 +10%
$4000 +15%
$5000+ +20%

For example, a cumulative order value of $3400 on the month of January 2019 will provide partners an additional 10% discount for the entire month of February 2019 on top of the 15% discount and weekly discount (if applied).

Weekly Discounts

Additional discount for the week is applied based on the previous week's cumulative order value.

Previous Week's Cumulative Order Value Additional Discount
$1000 +7.5%
$2000+ +15%

For example, a cumulative order value of $1600 on the week of 28 January - 03 February 2019 will provide partners an additional 10% discount for the entire week of 04 - 10 February 2019 on top of the 15% discount and monthly discount (if applied).

Free Priority Upgrade

Orders from partners will automatically be prioritised. This means that as soon as the order is paid, 3D printing of the order will start regardless of the queue.

Normally, 20% and 40% additional charges are applied for orders that are 'priority' and 'urgent', respectively. Orders from partners are treated as 'priority' and 'urgent' for free.

Example Scenario

Using the examples above, we'll demonstarte how these discounts can stack up.

Assuming a cumulative order value of $3400 on the month of January 2019. This will provide partners an additional 10% discount for the entire month of February 2019.

And assuming a cumulative order value of $1600 on the week of 28 January - 03 February 2019. This will provide partners an additional 7.5% discount for the entire week of 04 - 10 February 2019.

As a result, orders sent and paid for the week of 04 - 10 February 2019 will have a total discount of 32.5% coming from the 15% automatic discount, 10% monthly discount and 7.5% weekly discount.

Becoming a Partner

Becoming a partner is simple! You just need a minimum average monthly cumulative order value of $1,000. This can be based on your previous order history with us or based on a forecast.
